Race Team

Approached by a long time acquaintance who was currently
working amongst a team of students who formed the Ontario Tech Racing Team,
Gabriel set out to create a set of renders fit to represent the
teams current progress on they're Formula Student electric Racing machine.

Architectural Work

Approached by his former lecturer, who wished to
have his house redesigned with a more modern aesthetic. He set out
with the simplistic goal of doing the least work while reaping the
greatest reward as following with the teachings of Frank Lloyd
Wright "The architect should strive continually to simplify; the
ensemble of the rooms should then be carefully considered that
comfort and utility may go hand in hand with beauty."

Fantasy Pieces

In my past time I occasionally dabble in creating fantastical pieces,
places I'd like to see exist, places which arent totally implausible but
as far as I am aware dont exist From dungeon esc environments with beautiful Sakura pieces
as theres centre's to the occasional mountain top islands and meadows with ominous yet peaceful monoliths
